 📸 Lensia

## Smart Photography Booking & Gallery Management Platform

Lensia is a comprehensive platform that connects Clients, Photographers, Venue Owners, and Administrators in a unified ecosystem designed specifically for the photography industry.
The platform manages the complete photography workflow from discovering photographers and venues, booking sessions, handling payments, delivering galleries, managing revisions, collecting reviews, and monitoring post-session quality.
Lensia aims to modernize photography business operations and provide a seamless experience for all stakeholders involved in the photography process.

# 🎯 Project Vision

Traditional photography booking workflows are often fragmented across social media platforms, messaging applications, spreadsheets, and manual payment methods.
Lensia centralizes the entire process into a single platform that enables users to:

- Discover photographers and venues.
- Book photography sessions efficiently.
- Manage payments and deposits.
- Deliver and review galleries digitally.
- Request edits and revisions.
- Share galleries securely.
- Monitor post-session quality.
- Build a photography-focused community.

# 👥 User Roles

The system consists of four primary roles:

## Client

Users who search for photographers and venues and book photography sessions.

## Photographer

Professional photographers who manage portfolios, bookings, galleries, revisions, and client interactions.

## Venue Owner

Owners of photography venues available for reservation through the platform.

## Administrator

System managers responsible for approvals, moderation, monitoring, reporting, and quality assurance.

# 📅 Booking System

Lensia supports two booking methods.


## Method 1 — Photographer First

The client:

1. Selects a photographer.
2. Views available dates and times.
3. Chooses session duration.
4. Selects a suitable time slot.
5. Receives venue recommendations for that slot.
6. Chooses either:
   - A venue listed in Lensia.
   - An external location selected manually through the map.

### Payment

- Photographer deposit payment.
- Venue deposit payment (if a Lensia venue is selected).


## Method 2 — Full Session Booking

The client:

1. Selects a session date.
2. Selects a session time.
3. Selects a session type.
4. Receives recommended available photographers.
5. Receives recommended available venues.
6. Completes the booking.

### Payment

- Photographer deposit.
- Venue deposit if applicable.
